[ Memory Space: Chinatown AR ]

with Professor Scott Fisher @ USC's Mobile and Environmental Media Lab (MEML) and friends :)

In collaboration with [Metro Art, The Huntington-USC Institute on California and the West, the Chinese Historical Society of Southern California, and the Huntington Library].

A closer look (and a dev view) of two of the experiences: Peacock Alley and Sang Yuen Store.

  • Webapp in HTML, CSS, JavaScript (on Niantic's 8th Wall)
  • WebAR experiences with A-Frame, 8th Wall modules, and VPS waypoints
  • InWorld API for conversing with a peacock
  • Places volumetric videos of actors and custom 2D/3D assets on the screen, relative to the positioning of scanned waypoints around LA's Union Station
